Welcome to Holy Grace Academy

Holy Grace Academy is an English medium co-educational CBSE Residential School founded in 2000 with 135 students, 15 staff on roll and has consistently grown into a big family of around 3000 students and 300 staff as on today. The Holy Grace Academy CBSE School imparts international standards by adopting the latest methodologies, implementing innovative measures and ideas. From its inception onwards the School was successful in retaining its reputation as an educational institution with a difference by way of offering a host of features and training programs aimed at improving the academic, social, intellectual and behavioral skills of students. The School offers IIT and IAS foundation courses from Std. VII onwards. A research centre for learning disability also functions at the School. With a view to making education friendly and interesting to the first time school goers, the School has designed a Smart Kindergarten with latest technologies and innovative ideas to match international standards. This is a comprehensive academic schedule for Pre-Kg, LKG and UKG which reduces the burden on children, teachers and parents and makes learning a stimulating and rewarding experience.

Smart Kindergarten

An innovative kindergarten with digitalised class rooms gives new learning experiences to our tiny tots. It is a blend of conventional learning system and technology enabled inputs. We focus on holistic multiple intelligence development which includes logical, musical, linguistic, interpersonal, intra personal kinesthetic and visual intelligence of a child. The management has decided to restrict the number of LKG seats to 200 from 2015 -2016 as a part of "Quality Enhancement Initiative".
