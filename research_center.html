INCLUSIVE EDUCATION

We have a well organized Inclusive Department with a dedicated team working for the upliftment of the abilities and talents of the students. One prime motive is to teach the child the way she learns and to identify the difficulties the students come across and give a corrective mental and moral support to overcome it.


Holy Grace Academy is an English medium co-educational CBSE Residential School founded in 2000 with 135 students, 15 staff on roll and has consistently grown into a big family of around 3000 students and 300 staff as on today.
