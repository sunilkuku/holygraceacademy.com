Admission

Admission Overview

Parents seeking admissions for their little one for Pre Nursery is 2 years and 6 months during the year of joining. For Lower Kindergarten the age should be 3 years and 6 months plus during the year of joining. Online Registration for the admission can be done or else can contact the school Office. Mere Online registration does not ensure the admission. Admission to the school will be decided after the interaction session. The date and time of the Interaction Session will be intimated to you. Parents must accompany with their ward at the time of Interaction Session. Students seeking admission other than LKG, UKG & Class I will have to produce Transfer Certificate from the school last attended. Admission will be confirmed after the aptitude test ( Classes I – X) Documents to be submitted along with the Application Form: Attested copy of Birth Certificate, Original TC and Mark Sheet of the last examination passed at the previous school ( For Classes I- X)
